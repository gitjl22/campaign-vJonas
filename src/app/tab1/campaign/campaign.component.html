Campaign in a specific state

Look at the stats page to decide which state you want to focus your campaign on!

Your roll decides how much of an impact you wil have in a state, BUT...

Your campaigning is less effective the further the state is from your political stances.

Example:

A republican campaigning in a (+2 Dem) will subtract 2 from their roll.

A democrat campaigning in a (+2 Dem) will add 2 to their roll.
